    "- [A Survey of Learning Causality with Data: Problems and Methods](https://arxiv.org/abs/1809.09337) 2019 arxiv\n",
    "\n",
    "\n",
    "The era of big data provides researchers with convenient access to copious data. However, people often have little knowledge about it. The increasing prevalence of big data is challenging the traditional methods of learning causality because they are developed for the cases with limited amount of data and solid prior causal knowledge. This survey aims to **close the gap between big data and learning causality** with a comprehensive and structured review of traditional and frontier methods and a discussion about some open problems of learning causality. We begin with preliminaries of learning causality. Then we categorize and revisit methods of learning causality for the typical problems and data types. After that, we discuss the connections between learning causality and machine learning. At the end, some open problems are presented to show the great potential of learning causality with data."

    "- [A Survey on Causal Inference](https://arxiv.org/abs/2002.02770) AAAI 2020 tutorial\n",
    "\n",
    "因果推理是数十年来跨许多领域的重要研究主题，例如统计，计算机科学，教育，公共政策和经济学。如今，与随机对照试验相比，由于可用数据量大且预算要求低，因此根据观测数据估算因果关系已成为有吸引力的研究方向。伴随着快速发展的机器学习领域，各种用于观测数据的因果效应估计方法如雨后春笋般涌现。在这项调查中，我们对潜在结果框架（一种众所周知的因果推断框架）下的因果推断方法进行了全面回顾。 The methods are divided into two categories depending on **whether they require all three assumptions of the potential outcome framework or not**. For each category, both the traditional statistical methods and the recent machine learning enhanced methods are discussed and compared. The plausible applications of these methods are also presented, including the applications in advertising, recommendation, medicine and so on. 此外，还总结了常用的基准数据集以及开源代码，这些代码有助于研究人员和从业人员探索，评估和应用因果推理方法。"
